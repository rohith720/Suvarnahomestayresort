About Suvarna Homestay & Resort

Located on the peaceful Padubidri Murudi Brahmasthana Road, Suvarna Homestay & Resort is your premier destination for group getaways and family retreats. We blend modern luxury with a tranquil atmosphere, offering a spacious 4-bedroom villa, elegantly designed deluxe rooms, and versatile event spaces. Whether you’re here to celebrate or simply unwind, our property is crafted to provide you with the perfect balance of comfort, space, and serenity.
